FAQ

What is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu? +
Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu is a martial art and combat sport focused on grappling, ground fighting, and submission holds. It emphasizes technique and leverage over size and strength.
Do I need experience to start? +
No! Our Fundamentals program is designed for complete beginners. You'll learn at your own pace in a supportive environment.
What should I wear to my first class? +
Comfortable athletic clothing (rash guard and shorts). We also have gis available for rental.
How do I book a free trial class? +
