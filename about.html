Meet Our Instructors

Led by World Champions Bernardo Faria and Marcos Tinoco, DAY 1 provides a clear path for every stage of the journey — from your first class to advanced training.

Marcos Tinoco

Gi and NOGI World Champion, 4x Master World Champion, and a Black Belt under the legendary Marcelo Garcia. Renowned as one of the best instructors in the country, Marcos brings his wealth of experience to every class to help adults and kids reach their full potential. We're proud to be one of the few schools in the country with an instructor who is fully dedicated to teaching kids' classes.

Bernardo Faria

5x BJJ World Champion, IBJJF Hall of Fame member, and co-founder of BJJ Fanatics, the largest grappling instructional education platform in the world. Bernardo has dedicated his life to mastering and teaching Jiu-Jitsu, from winning world titles on the mat to helping students worldwide improve their game.

Hana Silva

A Black Belt under Marcelo Garcia, Hana Silva has dedicated her life to Jiu-Jitsu since 2007. She has a patient, inspiring approach to teaching that is perfect for beginners — and classes that build confidence on and off the mats.

Rayra Gracie

Proud member of the legendary Gracie family and the great-granddaughter of Jiu-Jitsu pioneer Carlos Gracie Sr. She has been involved with Jiu-Jitsu for her entire life and is passionate about teaching kids, helping them build confidence and character through age-appropriate games and self-defense techniques.
